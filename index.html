Anon for Web and Android

Anon is a unique app that aims to make it easy for users to send WhatsApp messages, make WhatsApp calls, and make WhatsApp payments without having to save the recipient's contact information. This concept comes from the need for people to be able to quickly send messages to unknown contacts while they are on the go.
One of the key features of Anon is that it allows users to message anyone on WhatsApp without having to save their contact information first. This is done by generating a unique link for each conversation, which can be shared with the recipient. This way, users can communicate with anyone they need to without having to add them to their contacts first.
Anon also allows users to make WhatsApp calls without having to save the recipient's contact information. This is done by generating a unique link for the call, which can be shared with the recipient. This way, users can make calls to anyone they need to without having to add them to their contacts first.
Another feature of Anon is that it allows users to make WhatsApp payments without having to save the recipient's contact information. This is done by generating a unique link for the payment, which can be shared with the recipient. This way, users can make payments to anyone they need to without having to add them to their contacts first.
Overall, Anon aims to provide a convenient and efficient way for users to communicate, call and make payments with anyone on WhatsApp without having to save their contact information. It's a perfect solution for people who often need to communicate with unknown contacts or those who prefer to keep their contacts list clean.

Doori - The Social Distancing Tracker

Doori is a social distance monitoring software that uses computer vision and machine learning techniques to detect and alert administrators of potential breaches in bio-bubbles. It utilizes the OpenCV library and YOLO v3 algorithm for real-time human detection. The goal of the software is to help enforce social distancing measures in areas where it is difficult to do so, such as crowded places like public transportation, airports, and other public spaces.
One of the main challenges facing governments around the world in the fight against COVID-19 is the lack of compliance with social distancing measures. The emergence of new variants of the virus, which are more contagious and potentially more dangerous than previous strains, has made this challenge even greater. In countries like India, where population density is high, it can be especially difficult to enforce social distancing measures.
Doori aims to address this challenge by providing a real-time monitoring solution that can help administrators quickly locate and address potential breaches in social distancing protocols. The software can be integrated into existing systems, such as CCTV cameras, to provide real-time alerts of potential breaches. It is important to note that Doori is not a replacement for other mitigation measures, but it can be an additional tool that administrators can use to help curb the spread of COVID-19.

Rural Steer

Our project aims to help farmers and gardeners make informed decisions about what crops to plant and how to fertilize them. By analyzing the health of the soil, we can determine which types of crops are best suited to the specific environment, and what fertilizers will be most effective in promoting growth and preventing disease.
The process begins with the user submitting a soil sample to a local agriculture lab. The lab will then perform a series of tests to assess the soil's pH level, nutrient content, and overall health. Once the results are received, our website will use that information to recommend specific crops and fertilizers that are best suited to the soil conditions.
One of the key features of our website is its user-friendly design. We understand that not everyone is an expert in agriculture, so we have made the process as simple and straightforward as possible. The website will provide clear and concise explanations of the soil test results, and will offer easy-to-understand recommendations for crops and fertilizers.
Our goal is to empower farmers and gardeners to take control of their soil health and to make better-informed decisions about how to grow their crops. By providing accurate and actionable information, we hope to improve the yields and overall health of crops, and ultimately contribute to a more sustainable food system.
